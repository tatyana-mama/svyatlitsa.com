Our Story

The room that breathes with you.

Svyatlitsa (svya·tlit·sa) is a Belarusian word for a light, airy, ceremonial room — the part of the home where guests are received, where windows face south, where the floor is swept with care.

We borrowed the idea and rebuilt it as a clinic. White oak. Linen curtains. Morning sunlight. No harsh white lamps, no plastic smell, no hurry. Just a room — and within it, quiet mastery.

The practice opens in September 2026 in Warsaw. Small by design — two procedure rooms, one philosophy.
